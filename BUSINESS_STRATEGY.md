# TaskFlow - Business & Product Strategy

## Product Overview

TaskFlow is a **professional task management and team collaboration platform** designed for:
- Small teams (2-20 people)
- Project managers
- Development teams
- Agencies & consultancies
- Freelancers with teams

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Modern UI/UX** - Beautiful, intuitive design
2. **Affordable** - Lower cost than Asana/Monday.com
3. **Self-hostable** - Option to self-host
4. **Customizable** - White-label available
5. **Real-time** - (future) Real-time collaboration
6. **Mobile Ready** - Responsive design
7. **Open Architecture** - Built with popular tech stack

## Revenue Model

### Freemium Tier (Free - Forever)
**Features:**
- Up to 5 projects
- Up to 10 tasks
- Basic team management (3 members)
- Email support
- Community forum

**Cost to Company:** Minimal infrastructure

### Professional Tier ($9.99/month)
**Features:**
- Unlimited projects
- Unlimited tasks
- Up to 20 team members
- Advanced analytics
- Priority support
- Custom branding (lite)
- API access

**Target:** Small teams, freelancers

### Enterprise Tier ($49.99/month)
**Features:**
- Everything in Professional
- Unlimited team members
- Custom workflows
- Advanced reporting
- SSO (Single Sign-On)
- Dedicated support
- White-label solution
- Custom integrations
- 99.9% SLA

**Target:** Medium to large companies

### White-Label License
**Price:** $299-999/month
**Features:**
- Full white-label
- Custom domain
- Custom branding
- Dedicated support
- Customizations

## Success Metrics

### User Metrics
- Daily Active Users (DAU)
- Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- Churn rate (target: < 5%)
- Retention rate (target: > 90% at 30 days)

### Business Metrics
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- LTV:CAC Ratio (target: > 3:1)

### Product Metrics
- Page load time (target: < 2s)
- Uptime (target: 99.9%)
- Feature usage
- User satisfaction (NPS score)
